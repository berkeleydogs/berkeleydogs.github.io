Why Inclusive Dog Spaces Matter

We advocate for safe, accessible dog parks in Berkeley that support pet owners and service dog handlers alike. From ADA-compliant spaces to service-dog-only hours, we envision a city where all dogs can thrive and be part of a more walkable, inclusive community.

  • Support mental and physical health
  • Encourage responsible dog ownership
  • Build community through inclusive spaces
  • Boost Berkeley’s national reputation as a dog-friendly city
