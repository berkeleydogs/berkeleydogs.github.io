Our Mission

Berkeley Dogs is a grassroots initiative advocating for the design and funding of inclusive, accessible, and thoughtfully planned dog parks throughout our city. These aren’t just amenities — they’re essential infrastructure that support public health, community connection, and disability access.

Be Part of the Movement

We believe that dogs and their humans deserve safe, inclusive, and vibrant spaces to connect. Join our community to support new dog park initiatives in Berkeley. We’ll send you updates on city proposals, volunteer opportunities, and public meetings.

Speak Up for Dog-Friendly Spaces

Your voice matters. Use our one-click email tool to contact Berkeley City Council and let them know you support modern, inclusive dog parks that serve every resident, including those with service animals.
